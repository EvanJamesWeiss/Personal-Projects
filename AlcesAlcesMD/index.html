Welcome!

You've landed on the homepage of the incomparable Moosesurgeon. And before you ask, yes, I am a moose who also performs surgery on meese (the correct plural of 'moose').

Wait a second, what's the deal with Alces Alces MD? That just sounds like nonsense! Well, there is indeed a meaning. As a friend pointed out one day, Alces Alces is the scientific name for the moose. The MD then comes from the surgeon portion of the name.

All kidding aside, this is a personal website developed by Evan Weiss. It's intention is to serve as a hub for personal projects, a digital resume, and testing my skills as a developer. After all, any self-respecting coder has their own website (or so I'm told).

Please enjoy your stay here. Links to my personal accounts can be found in the 'About' page. Additionally, feel free to try out any of the projects on the 'Projects' page.
